在openSUSE中安装MySQL要安装下面这些组件：

	libmysqlclient16

	libmysqlclient_r16

	libmysqld0

	libqt4-sql-mysql

	libreoffice-base-driver-mysql

	mysql-community-server

	mysql-community-server-client
